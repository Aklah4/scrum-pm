🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and a hashed version of your password. We never store your password in plain text. When you use ScrumPM, we store the project data you create — stories, sprints, risks, team members, and related content.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and maintain your ScrumPM workspace
  • To authenticate you and protect your account
  • To prevent duplicate accounts via email uniqueness checks
  • To respond to support and contact form submissions

3. Data storage

Your data is stored in MongoDB Atlas, a cloud-hosted database. Data is scoped strictly to your account — no other user can access your projects. We use compound unique indexes to enforce this at the database level.

4. Cookies and sessions

ScrumPM uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie is HTTP-only (not accessible to JavaScript), uses SameSite=Lax to prevent cross-site request forgery, and is Secure when served over HTTPS.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We use third-party infrastructure (MongoDB Atlas, Railway) to run the platform — these providers have their own privacy policies.

6. Data deletion

You can delete any project from the Projects page. To delete your account entirely, contact us at the address below and we will remove all associated data within 30 days.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via the Contact Us page.
