Everything you need

The complete Scrum toolkit

Every feature you need to run Scrum properly from the first story to the final retro.

📋

Product Backlog

Write user stories in the proper format, set priorities, assign story points, and organise them into sprints with a single click.

⚡

Sprint Planning

Create sprints with names, dates, and goals. Assign stories directly from the backlog and track velocity across sprints.

🗂️

Kanban Board

Visualise sprint progress with a To Do → In Progress → Done board. Move stories forward and backward with one click.

✅

Story Sub-tasks

Break each user story into concrete dev tasks. Tick them off from both the backlog and the sprint board as you build.

📊

Live Analytics

Charts for sprint completion, backlog health, story point distribution, phase progress, and risk breakdown all live.

⚠️

Risk Register

Log project risks, assign high/medium/low severity, and track mitigation plans so nothing falls through the cracks.

🔁

Sprint Retrospective

Run structured retros with What Went Well, What to Improve, and Action Items columns to keep your team growing.

👥

Team, RACI & Comms

Manage team members, stakeholder matrices, RACI charts, and communication plans everything a PM needs in one place.

🎯

Definition of Done

Define and track your team's DoD criteria. Stories only close when every acceptance criterion is met.
