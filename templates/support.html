🛟 Support

How can we help?

Answers to common questions. If you can't find what you need, reach out directly.

How do I create a sprint?

Go to Sprint Config in the tab bar. At the top of the page you'll find the Sprints panel — enter a name, start date, end date, and goal, then click + Create Sprint.

How do I assign stories to a sprint?

Open the Backlog page. Each story row has a sprint dropdown in the Sprint column. Select the sprint from the dropdown and it saves instantly.

Why can't I see my stories on the Sprint Board?

Stories only appear on the Sprint Board if they have been assigned to a sprint. Use the sprint dropdown on the Backlog page to assign them.

How do I add sub-tasks to a story?

On the Backlog page, click the ▶ arrow on the left of any story row to expand it. You'll see a task list where you can add, check, and delete tasks.

How do I switch between projects?

Click the ScrumPM logo in the top-left navbar to go back to your project list, then select a different project.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us using the form below and we'll remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.
