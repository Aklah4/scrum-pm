🏢 About us

Built for teams that ship

ScrumPM was built to give agile teams a single, focused place to plan, track, and ship — without the bloat of enterprise tools or the chaos of spreadsheets.

Our mission

To make Scrum accessible to every team, regardless of size. We believe good project management shouldn't require a dedicated administrator or a six-figure budget. ScrumPM gives you everything you need to run proper Scrum from day one — free to start, simple to use, powerful enough to scale.

🎯 Product-first

Every feature we build serves one goal: helping your team deliver better software, faster.

🔒 Private by design

Your project data is yours. Every project is scoped to your account and never shared across users.

⚡ Built on modern tech

Flask, MongoDB Atlas, and Railway — fast, reliable infrastructure that scales with your team.

🌍 Remote-ready

Browser-based, no installs, no VPNs. Your whole team can access ScrumPM from anywhere.
